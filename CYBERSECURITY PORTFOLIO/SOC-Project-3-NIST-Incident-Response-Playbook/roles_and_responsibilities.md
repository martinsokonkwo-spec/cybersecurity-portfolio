# Roles & Responsibilities

## SOC Tier 1 Analyst
- Monitor and triage security alerts
- Perform initial investigation
- Document findings and escalate incidents

## SOC Tier 2 Analyst
- Perform deep-dive analysis
- Lead containment and remediation actions
- Coordinate with IT and system owners

## Incident Response Lead
- Oversee incident handling
- Approve escalation and recovery decisions
- Communicate with management and stakeholders

## IT / System Owners
- Support containment and system recovery
- Implement remediation actions
