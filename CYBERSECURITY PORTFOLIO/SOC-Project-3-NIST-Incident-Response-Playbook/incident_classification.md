# Incident Classification & Severity

## Severity Levels

### Low
- Failed attacks with no impact
- No sensitive assets involved

### Medium
- Repeated attack attempts
- Targeting of sensitive users or systems
- No confirmed compromise

### High
- Successful unauthorized access
- Privileged account compromise
- Exposure of sensitive data

## Severity Determination Factors
- User role and privilege level
- Asset sensitivity
- Evidence of successful access
- Potential business impact

## Governance & Risk Alignment
- Incident severity supports risk-based decision making
- Classification informs escalation and resource allocation
- Documentation supports audit, compliance, and post-incident review
