## Incident Summary
Multiple authentication-related incidents were analyzed, including a brute-force
attempt and a confirmed privileged account compromise.

## What Worked Well
- Early detection through SIEM and log monitoring
- Structured escalation procedures
- Clear documentation of findings and actions

## Areas for Improvement
- Strengthen authentication controls for privileged accounts
- Improve alert tuning to reduce time to detection
- Expand monitoring for lateral movement indicators

## Recommended Control Updates
- Enforce multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Implement stricter account lockout policies
- Regular review of privileged account activity
